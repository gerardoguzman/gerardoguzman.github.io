Sonnet

This is a Sonnet that I wrote for my tenth grade English class. It is about my everyday life during the school day. I write about what I do at school and what I do when I get home. I feel that it really brought out my poetic side and I had fun writing it.

The Life of Gerardo

Wake up at seven o'clock,to get ready
Teeth brushed, clothes on, and ready to takeoff
Can not wait to see my little Eddie
I truly hate school, but it will payoff
Trying to get sleep in advisory
Bell rings, time to begin my awful day
Need help with sonnet, head to library
Earbuds in ear listening to Pompeii
Last class of the day; Mr. Carcido
Start taking notes and already tired
Assessments shot at me like torpedos
Last bell rings at last same time as prior
In car heading back home just want to sleep
Open bedroom door and start counting sheep.
